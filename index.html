Our Mission

We believe Women have so much to add to the global conversation of Feminism and now is our time to have their voices heard! Our mission is expose the gender injustices in places around the world. Also, to inspire others to come together and fight to change sterotypical "norms". People need to know that supporting women rights isn't just a "woman thing" it's a people thing. We wish to define, establish, and achieve the political, economic, personal, and social equality of the sexes.
